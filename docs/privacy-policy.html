BetterKnightsUI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

BetterKnightsUI is a browser extension that enhances the appearance of the UCF myUCF course search interface. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

BetterKnightsUI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension does not use analytics, tracking, or cookies.

Data Storage

The extension stores only your toggle preference (enabled/disabled) and a launch counter using your browser's built-in storage API. This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

The extension queries the RateMyProfessors website to display professor ratings within the course search results. These requests are made directly from your browser to RateMyProfessors. We do not proxy, log, or store any of this data.

Permissions

The extension requests access to UCF myUCF pages and RateMyProfessors solely to provide its core functionality. It does not access any other websites or browsing data.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
