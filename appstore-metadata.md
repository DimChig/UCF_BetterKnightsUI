## Description
Tired of the outdated 2006 design on myUCF? BetterKnightsUI transforms the UCF course search experience with a clean, modern interface.

Features:
- Redesigned course search tables with clear, organized layouts
- Color-coded day-of-week badges for quick schedule scanning
- RateMyProfessors ratings displayed directly in search results
- Enhanced search form with a cleaner, more intuitive layout
- One-click toggle to enable or disable the extension

Works on the myUCF class search page (Student Self Service > Enrollment > Add Classes > Search).

Note: After installing, go to Safari > Settings > Extensions > BetterKnightsUI and grant permission to the UCF and RateMyProfessors websites for full functionality.
